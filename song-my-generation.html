My Generation

From My Generation (1965)

History

An anthem for rebellious youth, 'My Generation' captures the frustration and anger of the Mod subculture. Its stuttering vocals and aggressive instrumentation made it a defining song of the era.

Key: G
Difficulty: Intermediate
